Cold-Fermented GF Pizza

Caputo Fioreglut · Cast Iron · 550°F
Yield 2 × 11.5" pizzas
Serves 4
Prep 30 min
Ferment 12–24 h
Bake 9–11 min

Ingredients

Dough
400 gCaputo Fioreglut flour
320 gwater (80% hydration)
8 gfine salt
2 ginstant yeast
15 golive oil
Toppings (per pizza)
2–3 tbspdrained crushed tomatoes
100–110 glow-moisture mozzarella
1–2 tspolive oil (for pan)
If your fridge runs warm (>40°F), drop yeast to 1.5 g.

Dough Method

1. Mix

Combine flour, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.

Add water + olive oil.

Stir until fully hydrated.

Knead gently 2–3 minutes until smooth.

Rest 15 minutes (important for GF structure).

2. Initial Rise

Cover and let sit at room temp 30–45 minutes.

3. Cold Ferment

Lightly oil bowl, cover tightly, refrigerate 12–24 hours. 18 hours is ideal.

Next Day

1. Warm Up

Remove dough 2 hours before baking.

2. Divide & Rest

Divide into 2 balls (~375 g each). Gently round. Cover and rest 45–90 minutes until relaxed.

The dough should feel: soft, slightly puffy, extensible but not sticky.

Shaping (Critical for GF)

  • Lightly oil your hands
  • Place dough on parchment
  • Press from center outward
  • Leave rim slightly thicker
  • Do not toss or stretch in air
  • Target ~⅛" thick center

Baking

1. Preheat

  • Cast-iron pizza pan on middle rack
  • Oven to 550°F
  • Heat pan 30–40 minutes (non-negotiable)

2. Load

  • Remove hot pan carefully
  • Brush with 1–2 tsp olive oil
  • Slide dough (on parchment) onto pan
  • Remove parchment after 60–90 seconds

3. Top (Restrained)

  • 2–3 tbsp drained crushed tomatoes
  • 100–110 g low-moisture mozzarella
  • Keep toppings light

4. Bake

  • 9–11 minutes total
  • Rotate once halfway
  • Check underside at 9 minutes
  • Optional: broiler 30–60 seconds for top color

Pull When

  • Bottom is deep golden with dark freckles
  • Center feels firm when lifted
  • Cheese bubbling but not watery
  • Rim lightly blistered

Rest 2 minutes before slicing.

Troubleshooting

Pale bottom Longer pan preheat or +1–2 min bake
Soft center Less sauce next time or +1 min bake
Cracking while shaping Dough still too cold — wait longer

Expected Texture

Crisp base Tender interior Clean slice structure Not foldable — structural physics, not technique
