Instant Pot Chicken Stock

Fresh Drumsticks · Defatted · Reduced · Unseasoned
Yield ~2 qt (8 cups)
Serving 1 cup (240 ml)
Pressure 70 min
Reduction ~30%
Total ~24 h (incl. chill)

Ingredients

2.5 lbsbone-in skin-on chicken drumsticks (1135 g)
~10 cupswater (to 6 qt Instant Pot max fill line)
No salt, vinegar, vegetables, or aromatics. This is a pure unseasoned base ingredient for logging purposes.

Method

Cook

Place drumsticks in 6 qt Instant Pot. Fill with water to max fill line.

Seal lid. High pressure, 70 minutes. Allow natural pressure release.

Remove chicken — reserve meat for separate use. Strain stock through mesh strainer.

Defat

Refrigerate stock overnight (or until fully chilled). A solid fat cap will form on top.

Remove and discard the fat cap. This removes nearly all rendered fat from the skin.

Reduce

Return defatted stock to pot. Simmer uncovered on stovetop until reduced by approximately 30%.

Final yield: ~2 quarts (8 cups). Transfer to 2 quart-size Ball jars.

The stock should gel when chilled. If it gels firmly, your protein content is at the higher end of the estimate. A loose gel means lower gelatin extraction — still good, but closer to the low end.

Nutrition (per 1 cup / 240 ml serving)

40 kcal
9 g Protein
0 g Carbs
0.5 g Fat
How these numbers were derived: Fresh skin-on drumsticks at high pressure for 70 minutes extract significant collagen/gelatin into the liquid — comparable sources report 6–10 g protein per cup for pressure-cooked bone broth from fresh chicken. The 30% reduction step concentrates this further. Defatting via cold fat-cap removal eliminates most rendered fat (~95%), leaving trace residual fat. The 9 g protein estimate assumes a firmly gelling stock; if yours is a loose gel, 6–7 g/cup is more realistic. Zero carbs because there are no vegetables or aromatics in the pot. There is no peer-reviewed study that directly measures the macros of this exact preparation — these estimates are triangulated from USDA FoodData Central entries for home-prepared chicken stock (FDC #172884: 2.5 g protein/cup pre-reduction with vegetables) adjusted upward for the higher bone-to-water ratio, no-vegetable dilution, pressure extraction, and 30% reduction. The meat is removed and logged separately — none of its macros are counted here.
