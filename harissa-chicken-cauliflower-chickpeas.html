Harissa Chicken with Cauliflower and Chickpeas

Sheet Tray · North African · One Pan
Serves4
MethodSheet Tray · 425°F
Total Time~65 min
Gluten-Free✓ Check harissa
Why sequencing matters on a sheet tray Cauliflower needs 15 minutes alone on a screaming hot tray to develop real char rather than steam. Chickpeas need to dry out to get crispy. The chicken goes in halfway through — its fat and harissa then drip onto the vegetables for the second half, which is where the flavor integration actually happens.

Harissa

Store-Bought 3–4 Tbsp harissa paste. Mina and DEA brands are reliably GF. Rose harissa (with dried rose petals) is a Tunisian variation — more floral and complex than standard. Worth trying if available.
Homemade 4 dried guajillo chilis (rehydrated) · 2 dried árbol chilis · 3 garlic cloves · 1 tsp cumin · 1 tsp coriander · ½ tsp caraway · 2 Tbsp olive oil · juice of ½ lemon · salt. Blend smooth. Makes ½ cup, refrigerates 2 weeks.

Ingredients

Chicken

3 lbbone-in skin-on chicken thighs
3–4 Tbspharissa paste (store-bought or homemade)
1 Tbspolive oil

Vegetables

1 large headcauliflower, cut into large florets
15 oz canchickpeas, drained, rinsed, and dried thoroughly
2 Tbspolive oil
1 tspground cumin
1 tspsmoked paprika
—salt and black pepper

Finish

handfulfresh cilantro or flat-leaf parsley
—lemon wedges
optionalplain Greek yogurt thinned with lemon juice as a drizzle
Chickpea drying is not optional. Wet chickpeas straight from the can will steam instead of crisp. Drain, rinse, then spread on a clean towel and pat completely dry — or leave them out uncovered for 30 minutes. This step is what separates crispy chickpeas from soft ones.

Method

1 — Preheat

Place a large sheet tray in the oven. Preheat to 425°F. The tray must be hot before anything goes on it — this is what creates bottom caramelization rather than steaming.

2 — Vegetables First

Toss cauliflower florets and dried chickpeas with 2 Tbsp olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Coat thoroughly.

Carefully remove hot tray from oven. Spread cauliflower and chickpeas in a single layer — don't crowd or they'll steam. Return to oven for 15 minutes.

3 — Add Chicken

While vegetables roast, coat chicken thighs thoroughly with harissa and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Season with salt. Every surface should be covered.

After 15 minutes, remove tray. Nestle chicken thighs skin-side up among the cauliflower and chickpeas. Return to oven.

Roast 30–35 minutes more until chicken skin is deep golden and blistered, cauliflower has charred edges, and chickpeas are crispy. Internal temp of chicken: 165°F.

4 — Finish

Scatter herbs over the tray. Serve directly with lemon wedges. If using yogurt drizzle, thin Greek yogurt with lemon juice and a pinch of salt until pourable.
